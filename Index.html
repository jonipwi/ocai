Welcome to OCAI

OCAI is a website that provides information and resources on organizational culture assessment.

We offer a variety of tools and resources to help you assess your organization's culture, identify areas for improvement, and develop a plan for change.

Whether you're a leader, manager, or employee, OCAI can help you create a more effective and productive organization.

About OCAI

OCAI was founded in 2023 by a team of experienced organizational development professionals.

We are passionate about helping organizations create cultures that are healthy, productive, and aligned with their values.

We believe that organizational culture is the foundation of success, and we are committed to providing the resources and tools that organizations need to create cultures that thrive.
